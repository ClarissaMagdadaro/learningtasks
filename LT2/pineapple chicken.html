    • Pineapple Chicken

    This was one of the first easy recipes I learnt to cook and can be mastered in just a few attempts!

    4 Servings

    Ingredients


    2 tsp canola or sesame oil
    1 (10-oz) package frozen broccoli (or stire-fry vegetable mix), thawed*1/2 C stir-fry sauce
    1/4 C pineapple juice, use reserved juice from canned pineapple
    1/4 tsp garlic powder
    1/4 tsp crushed red pepper (optional-use if you like it hot!)
    1 (15-oz) can pineapple chunks or tidbits,drained (reserve the juice!)
    2 C diced cooked chicken or 2 (10-oz) cans chicken breast, drained and flaked

    Method

    1. Heat the oil in the large skillet over medium high heat. Add all ingredients EXCEPT the pineapple and chicken. Cook and stir until heated through 5-6mins.
    2. Add pineappple and chicken; cook another 2 minutes.
    Serve over instant brown rice or whole wheat pasta.
    *Thaw frozen vegetables in the microwave or by holding the package under cold runnung water for several minutes.
